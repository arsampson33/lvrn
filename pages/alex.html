About

Singer and songwriter Alex Vaughn is a new face in the R&B scene, with hopes of leaving a long-lasting impact on the music industry.

In October of last year, Vaughn signed a deal with Love Renaissance (LVRN)/Interscope, then released her first single “Mirage,” a few months after. Since then, the songstress has continued to build an increasing buzz with her peers and supporters alike.
